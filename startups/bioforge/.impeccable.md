## Design Context

### Users
- Creators, influencers, freelancers, small businesses, musicians, artists
- Anyone who needs a personal bio/link page for social media
- Users range from Gen Z (TikTok, IG) to professionals (LinkedIn, Twitter)

### Brand Personality
**Bold, playful, personal.** This is NOT a corporate tool—it's for people expressing their identity.
- 3 words: **Expressive, Dynamic, Memorable**
- Should feel like a personal brand, not a SaaS product

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Dark mode first** with vibrant accent colors
- **Glassmorphism** as primary decorative element (purposeful, not decorative)
- **Micro-animations** on hover/tap (scale, glow, subtle movement)
- **Bold typography** - distinctive display font paired with clean body
- **Gradient accents** - not text gradients, but subtle background washes
- **Card-based layout** but with depth (shadows, borders, glow effects)

### Design Principles
1. **Personal expression first** - users should feel their page is THEIRS
2. **Delightful interactions** - every tap should feel responsive and fun
3. **Visual hierarchy** - clear what's clickable, what's important
4. **Performance matters** - fast loads, smooth transitions
5. **Mobile-native** - most users view on phone

### Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Backend:** Express.js (separate server)
- **Database:** Firestore
- **CDN:** Cloudinary (free tier) or UploadThing
- **Auth:** Firebase Auth

### Reference Vibes
- Like Carrd.co but more modern/dynamic
- Linktree alternative but with actual design freedom
- Think: Notion-style minimal mixed with gaming aesthetic
