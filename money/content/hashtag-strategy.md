# Hashtag Strategy

## Primary Hashtags (Use Every Post)
- #BuildInPublic - Core audience
- #SaaS - Industry
- #Startup - Startup ecosystem
- #AI - High volume, reach

## Secondary Hashtags (Rotate)
### For AI Content:
- #AI #ArtificialIntelligence #MachineLearning #ChatGPT #PromptEngineering #AITools #TechTips

### For Automation:
- #Automation #NoCode #Productivity #Workflow #Efficiency

### For Side Hustle:
- #SideHustle #Entrepreneur #IndieHacker #Founder #StartupLife #Grind

### For Journey/Wins:
- #Journey #Growth #Learning #Progress #Wins

## Hashtag Rules
1. **3-5 per post** - Too many looks spammy
2. **Mix of sizes:** 1 big (#AI), 2 medium (#BuildInPublic), 1-2 niche
3. **Put at end** or naturally in text
4. **Don't force** - only use relevant ones

## Trending Combo Strategy
- Morning posts: #MondayMotivation
- Wins: Add date-specific if relevant
- Tech: #TechTwitter #DevTwitter

## Best Times to Post Summary

### Global Tech Audience (PST)
| Rank | Time | Engagement |
|------|------|------------|
| 1 | 9 AM Tue-Thu | Highest |
| 2 | 12-1 PM | High |
| 3 | 5-7 PM | Medium-High |
| 4 | 8 AM Mon/Fri | Medium |

### Worst Times
- 11 PM - 6 AM (dead zone)
- 2-4 PM (afternoon dip)

### Pro Tips
- Post 30 min before peak times
- Test your audience, adjust accordingly
- Weekends = lower volume but higher individual engagement
- Consistency > viral moments
